The Problem: Cash Flow Hemorrhaging

The 90-Day Death Cycle
Subcontractors complete work but wait 60-120 days for payment. 30% of construction businesses fail due to cash flow, not lack of work.
Government PDF Hell
$13T in government contracts trapped in 500-page RFPs. Companies spend $50K-$300K per proposal with 90% failure rate.
Labor Arbitrage Failure
General Contractors lose bids because they can't find crews. Subcontractors sit idle. No real-time matching exists for skilled trades.
Permitting Bottleneck
Projects delayed 3-18 months by manual plan reviews. $200B in annual permitting costs, 80% administrative waste.

The Solution: 7-Layer Infrastructure

Layer 7
Government Intelligence
Bloomberg Terminal for gov contracts. AI RFP discovery, automated proposal writing, compliance scoring.
Layer 6
AI Intelligence
Palantir-style analytics. Predict delays, optimize crews, prevent fraud before it happens.
Layer 5
Labor Marketplace
Uber for trades. Real-time matching of vetted, insured crews to commercial projects.
Layer 4
Embedded Finance
Smart escrow, instant payroll, material financing. We hold the float.
Layer 3
Compliance Engine
Automated KYC, AML, lien waivers, insurance verification. Regulatory shield.
Layer 2
Payment Rails
Hybrid open/closed loop. ACH, RTP, FedNow, internal ledger, USDC settlement.
Layer 1
Vertical SaaS
Project management, blueprint AI, permit automation. The wedge.
