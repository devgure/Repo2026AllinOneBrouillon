The Monopoly Opportunity

The single most dangerous position to occupy in the AI economy.

The Universal Semantic Layer

The middleware that translates any database query into natural language and back.

The Problem

Data is locked in silos (Salesforce, SAP, Oracle). AI cannot easily access it.

The Solution

A universal API that understands any schema and translates it to natural language.

The Moat

Once embedded, companies cannot leave. You control access to all corporate data.

Why This is Dangerous

  • You become the "Google" of enterprise data.
  • Every AI agent needs your layer to function.
  • Network effects: More data = better translation = more customers.
  • Potential to tax every transaction in the digital economy.
